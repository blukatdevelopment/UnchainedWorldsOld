# Book Of Maladies

## Curses

### Malignant Lycanthropy
You gain the Lycanthrope Form feature from the lycanthrope class. If you received lycanthropy from a bite, your form mathes that of the biter. If not, roll randomly.
```
+------+----------+
| Roll |   Form   |
+------+----------+
|    1 | Werewolf |
|    2 | Wererat  |
|    3 | Werebear |
+------+----------+
```
